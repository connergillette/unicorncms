Bio
I am a third-year student at the University of Washington. I grew up in Sammamish, Washington and went to Skyline High School, where I co-founded and co-captained (and in 2017, coached) the Ultimate Frisbee Club. I like playing Dota and learning new skills in my free time. I consider myself a quick learner and easily self-motivated to learn new skills and be productive.

Ever since I was a little kid I've loved working with computers and learning about different ways to use them. In elementary school I taught myself how to program in C / C++, and in middle school I started looking at C++ and HTML / CSS.

Lately I've been exploring interests in web design and web app development, and my work at efelle creative has helped hone my skills in that area.

Since the fall of 2016, the Dota club at UW has been my main extracurricular project. It has since grown into a partnership with the Washington Gaming Association and three competitive teams (one of which is affiliated with Washington eSports). I'm very proud of how far the club has gone, and am working to make the club better for as many Dota players as possible.

I constantly like to be learning new things, whether it be something that will help me academically, in my career, or in general. I hope to pursue opportunities in the future that will help me grow as a person and do meaningful work in the process.
