QuickQuiz by Knowledge Anywhere

How smart is your team?

QuickQuiz allows you to build and deploy quizzes to your team via Slack. These quizzes can be used to reinforce and confirm knowledge on product, policies, technology, company direction, market trends, etc.

Installation

Click on the "Add to Slack" button in the banner and follow the instructions to add QuickQuiz to your Slack team. You can also visit our App Directory page and install QuickQuiz from there.

Support

More instructions can be found on our Zendesk. If that doesn't answer your questions, please contact support@knowledgeanywhere.com with any questions.

An administrator can:

  • Build a new quiz in a few minutes
  • Assign users and channels to a quiz
  • Have flexibility in the number of answer choices
  • Get a list of user results for quizzes they create

A user can:

  • Answer quiz questions in Slack
  • Receive private results
  • Get instant feedback on the correctness of their answers
  • Get a summary of results
  • Get a notification if there are quizzes assigned to them

Commands

/quiz start Starts the next quiz assigned to you.
/quiz list assigned Lists all quizzes available for you to take.
/quiz list created Lists all quizzes created by you.
/quiz results "Quiz Name" Lists the scores of everybody who has taken the provided quiz. (You must be the creator of the quiz)
/quiz clear "Quiz Name" Allows everybody who has already taken the quiz to take it again. (You must be the creator of the quiz)
/quiz create Starts or resumes a quiz builder.
/quiz assign "Quiz Name" @username Assigns a user to a quiz. (You must be the creator of the quiz)
/quiz assign "Quiz Name" #channel Assigns a channel to a quiz. (You must be the creator of the quiz)
/quiz cancel Cancels a quiz builder and deletes its quiz.
/quiz help Displays all possible QuickQuiz commands.
